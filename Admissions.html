TUITION

Clearview Heights High School believes in providing quality education accessible to all students regardless of financial background. The annual tuition fee for attending Clearview Heights High School is set at a reasonable rate of $10,000 per year. However, the school offers various financial assistance programs and scholarships to ensure that no student is denied access to education due to financial constraints.

SCHOLARSHIPS

Merit-Based Scholarships: Clearview Heights High School offers merit-based scholarships to recognize and reward academic excellence, leadership, and extracurricular achievements. These scholarships are awarded to students who demonstrate outstanding performance in academics, sports, arts, community service, and other areas. The amount of the scholarship varies based on the student's achievements and may cover a portion or all of the tuition fees.

Need-Based Financial Aid: In addition to merit-based scholarships, Clearview Heights High School provides need-based financial aid to students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. The financial aid program is designed to assist families with demonstrated financial need in covering tuition expenses. Eligibility for need-based financial aid is determined through a thorough evaluation of the family's financial situation.

Athletic Scholarships: Clearview Heights High School recognizes the importance of athletics in a well-rounded education and offers athletic scholarships to talented student-athletes. These scholarships are awarded to students who excel in sports and demonstrate potential for success at the high school level. Athletic scholarships may cover a portion of the tuition fees and are renewable based on the student's performance and contribution to the school's athletic programs.
